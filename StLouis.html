St. Louis, MO

St. Louis is a beautiful city. There are tons to do here. Although you cannot fly through the St. Louis Arch, there still is a lot of very cool shots you can get here. One of my favorite places to visit was on the Illinois side of the river. There is an island off of I-270 called Chouteau Island that is used mainly for the fishing spot right on the Mississippi River. If you are headed out that way, check this spot out!
