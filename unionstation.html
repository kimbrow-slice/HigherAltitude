Union Station | Liberty Memorial

One of Kansas Cities most visited locations would be Union Station. Whether you are visiting from out of town or just wanting to go on a nice walk. Union Station has a beautiful view of downtown Kansas City. There is so much to do here from a movie theatre, escape room, and even the Amtrak station.
